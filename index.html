About Us

Zeliant Hotspot, a proud division of Zeliant IT Solutions, is committed to providing reliable internet and cutting-edge security services and communities. Headquartered in Suba, Samboan, Cebu, we specialize in high-speed internet though partnerships with Globe and PLDT, as well as professional CCTV installations, ensuring seamless connectivity and safety for our clients.

Our services extended from Suba to Ginatilan, Oslob, Malabuyoc, Alegria, Central Cebu, and Northern Cebu, reaching a wide network of communities. At Zeliant Hotspot, we strive to empower communities by bridging the digital divide and fostering growth growth through technology.
